Accomodation

There are of course a great many places to stayin in Ireland and you are welcome to stay at any of them. However, to help you narrow down your search, have have listed some suggestions below. We will be running buses to Derry and locally to and from the venue, if you stay further afield you may need to sort your own transport

Staying in Derry

Derry is the closest City to the wedding venue and has an array of hotel options including (but not limited to):

The Bishop's Gate

The City Hotel

The White Horse Hotel

Water Foot Hotel

Travelodge Derry

We will be running a bus to and from the Wedding from the Derry City Centre - for your convience

Staying in Ballyliffin

If you would like to be closer to the wedding and stay in Ballyliffin, once again you have a number of choices at your disposal. There are a number of hotels and holiday homes in the area inlcuding (but not limited to):

Ballyliffin Hotel

Strand Hotel

Ballyliffin Lodge and Spa Hotel

Ballyliffin TownHouse

There are plenty more options on AirBnb around the areas as well
